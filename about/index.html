About

Studio Thomas Willow is a response to the ever-developing technologies that direct us away from the powers of nature and our own introspection. The art created at Studio Thomas Willow emulates the movement, sound and light of the natural world and aims to bring people back to a slower, more natural pace of life.

The studio is driven to promote environmental and lifestyle change. Restoring the balance between nature & technology through captivating artworks.
